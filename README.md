This dataset represents pharmaceutical product sales across different locations, sales channels, and sales managers. It helps analyze performance, profitability, and demand patterns.
table colums include:  date, sales managers, location, product, and sales channel, country, city, salesperson names, product names, categories, quantity, price, and sales.
